CARPARTSRADAR Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 9, 2026 · Applies to the carpartsradar.com website and the CarPartsRadar iOS app.

The short version

CarPartsRadar does not require an account and does not sell or share your personal data. Your garage, watchlist, and recent searches live on your own device. The iOS app contains no analytics or advertising SDKs.

What we process, and why

  • Search queries (vehicle year/make/model/trim and part name) are sent to our servers to fetch live listings from eBay and vehicle data from NHTSA. They are not linked to your identity.
  • Part photos — if you use "Identify from a photo," the photo is sent to our server and forwarded to Google's Gemini API solely to name the part. We do not store your photos.
  • VIN scans — text recognition runs on your device. Only the 17-character VIN itself is sent to our server and NHTSA to decode your vehicle. Your photo never leaves the device.
  • Price-alert email — if you subscribe to a price alert on the website, we store the email address you provide in order to send that alert. You can unsubscribe at any time.
  • Website analytics — the website uses PostHog for aggregate usage analytics. The iOS app includes no analytics.

What stays on your device

Your garage (saved vehicles, optional VIN and mileage), watchlist, recent searches, and preferences are stored locally on your device (browser storage on the website, app storage on iOS). Deleting the app or clearing site data removes them.

Third-party services

  • eBay — listing data and purchases. Tapping "Buy" takes you to eBay; their privacy policy applies there. Listing links may be affiliate links.
  • NHTSA vPIC — vehicle year/make/model and VIN decoding (a U.S. government service).
  • Google Gemini — part-photo identification, as described above.
  • Wikipedia/Wikimedia — vehicle photos shown in your garage.
